Companies using AppUpgrade™

Your team focuses on shipping product, we'll make sure users use it!
  • Ensure your users have access to latest features
    Power-users are often the last ones to get a product update. Why? Because they keep your app open all day and don't refresh their tabs. AppUpgrade™ solves this problem and makes sure that all users get product updates ASAP.
  • Eliminate bugs related to incompatible app & api versions
    App & Api version incompatibility is one of the most common vectors for bugs that engineering teams face today. When QA'ing releases its too easy to forget to test server-side changes against older app versions. AppUpgrade™ solves this problem and effectively eliminates the potential for this class of bug to occur.
  • Educate your users on the latest features & changes to end-users
    Did you add a new feature or make a change to your webapp that you want to share with your users? AppUpgrade™ is a great way to educate your users with our release notes feature.
  • Visibility into what versions of your app are still being used
    AppUpgrade™ lets you see what versions of your app are still being used. What's more, it will alert your team once a version of your app hasn't been used for a certain amount of time so that you can safely delete any deprecated and unsupported apis.
